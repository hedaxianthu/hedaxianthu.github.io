About

I am a PhD Candidate at the School of Vehicle and Mobility (SVM), Tsinghua University (2024-Present), advised by Prof. Dr. Yangjun Zhang. During my undergraduate period in Tsinghua University, I obtained my B.S. in Theoretical and Applied Mechanics, and B.Eng. in Vehicle Engineering. My current research centers on the mechanism and modelling of ducted fan eVTOLs' propulsion and dynamics amidst various disturbances, including proximity and meteorogical hazards. Aside from academics, I lead my life as an amateur transportation photographer and frequent traveller.
